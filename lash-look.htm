Lash Look

You know how some styles of hair suit some face shapes but not others? Or how some make-up techniques, like creating a smoky eye, don’t necessarily work for everyone? Well, same goes for eyelash extensions– one style may suit one eye shape, but may not flatter someone with a different eye shape. And because you can’t exactly try on lash extensions, we created a few looks for you to choose. *Please note that this will be discussed during consultation to ensure it'll best suit you.

Lash Options

  • Synthetic
  • Silk
  • Faux-mink

These are made from a polished, acrylic material and are the firmest/sturdiest of all. In appearance, they are a little shinier or glossier than the mink or silk.

While they come in a wide variety of thicknesses and curls, and can be applied to look very natural, these are most popular with ladies wanting a more dramatic and made-up look. They hold their curl extremely well due to their firmness.

They are most suitable for those with healthy, dense lashes of their own. If your own lashes are very fine or weak, then we’d recommend silk or mink over synthetic, as they are slightly lighter.

These are the mid-weight type of lash extensions, and are finer and more flexible than the synthetic type. They can be made to look very natural or very glamorous, but not quite as dense/thick as the synthetic lashes. Because they are lighter weight, they are often a better option for your lashes if your own ones are weak or fine.

Like synthetic lashes, silk come in a variety of curls and lengths, though are a better option for a more natural look, as the curl is not generally as uniform or as pronounced as synthetic.

Glamour sets of silk lashes are one of the most popular options for those who are looking for lashes that are full and long yet still natural looking

Faux-mink lashes, which are man-made fibers are designed to imitate real mink fur. They are a very luxurious option for a fine, soft and silky touch. They however can lose their curl slightly when wet. Real mink lashes tend to lose their curl completely, thus why Lash Lab chooses to offer Faux mink lashes instead.

Faux-mink lashes are becoming very popular because they are so light and fine. A fabulous option for fine or weak lashes. It’s the ultimate luxury in lashes!
