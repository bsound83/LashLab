About Us

At Lash Lab we offer personalized Eyelash Extension services in a quiet, sophisticated setting that inspires confidence. With results in mind, discreet and knowledgeable services are performed exclusively by licensed and highly trained technicians. Our techs take pride in their work and have a passion for luscious lashes.

Lisa Soundara

Licensed Cosmetologist

With 11 years of experience, Lisa, has placed a mark on the Midwest's beauty industry. Lisa, also known as the "Lash Queen" has excelled in many aspects of the industry. She’s a certified Advanced Eyelash Extensionist, which sets her education apart. Lisa is also an educator for Lash Extensions and has traveled internationally to teach. Lisa is truly passionate about her work and is dedicated to superior service.

Pansy Flemming

Licensed Esthetician

Pansy has years of experience in the beauty industry in skin care as a client and Licensed Esthetician. She is also certified in AHA/BHA chemical peels and is a certified NovaLash eyelash extensionist. After obtaining her Minnesota State license in 2011, she also continues her advanced education in skin care at the International Dermal Institute. Her commitment in the skin care industry has gained her trust in clients and has since developed a great rapport.
